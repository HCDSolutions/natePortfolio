My UX Process

Empathize

I talk directly with users through interviews and surveys to understand their needs and challenges.

Define

I define the problem by aligning user needs with business goals and creating a clear direction for the team.

Ideate

I facilitate creative exercises that challenge assumptions and spark new thinking, then use quick sketches and prototypes to test and refine ideas.

Prototype

I build interactive prototypes to test concepts early, reduce risk, and refine the design through user feedback.

Test

I test solutions with users to measure usability, identify friction, and strengthen the experience before release.
